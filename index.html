What is the equity ratio?
The equity ratio is a financial ratio that indicates the relative proportion of the equity that is used to finance a company's assets.
How to calculate equity ratio?
To calculate equity ratio you should use the following formula:
equity ratio = total equity / total assets
example: company x's total assets are $94,000 and the total shareholder equity of the company is $37,000, calculate the equity ratio.
using the equity ratio the answer will be:
equity ratio = total equity / total assets
equity ratio = $37,000 / $94,000
equity ratio = 0.39
Why use equity ratio?
the equity ratio is used because it tells how much of the company's assets are funded by issuing stock and not by borrowing. It is also an indicator of a firm's financial stability in the long run.
